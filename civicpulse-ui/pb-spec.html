Procedural Bites — Card Spec

How a PB card listens to the meeting and builds itself in real-time. Each stage shows what the card looks like as the meeting unfolds.

Stage 1 — Motion Detected
The system hears "I move that..." or "THAT Council..." from the transcript. A new PB card appears. The mover is highlighted as it's being identified from audio.
