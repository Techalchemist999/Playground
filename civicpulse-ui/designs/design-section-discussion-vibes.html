Section / Sub-item / Discussion / Motion — 10 Design Vibes

Exploring how to present the four-level hierarchy for each agenda section: Header (e.g. "4. ADOPTION OF MINUTES") → Sub-header (e.g. "4.1 Minutes of November 12, 2025") → Discussion box (optional, can be added) → Motion.

Section Header Sub-header Discussion (optional) Motion
1

Stacked White Cards

Each level is its own white card. Left-edge accent colour cues the hierarchy. Discussion is optional — "+ Add Discussion" button appears between sub-header and motion when empty.
4. ADOPTION OF MINUTES
4.1 Minutes of November 12, 2025 Special Council Meeting
Councillor Smith noted two typos on page 3. The corrections were accepted without objection.
MOTION:
THAT the minutes of the November 12, 2025 Special Council Meeting be approved as amended.
MOVED BY: Cllr Johnson · SECONDED BY: Cllr Patel
CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY
4.2 Minutes of November 19, 2025 Committee of the Whole
MOTION:
THAT the minutes of the November 19, 2025 Committee of the Whole be approved as circulated.
MOVED BY: Cllr Lee · SECONDED BY: Cllr Okafor
CARRIED
