Opposed Votes — 10 Design Vibes

Where the opposed voters live on the bottom-left of the motion/amendment/final-vote card (mirroring the disposition pill on the right). Rule: if a motion is carried (but not carried unanimously), show who opposed. Card shell, nesting, and pill style stay locked — this is the add-on for that empty left slot.

1

Plain inline text

Cleanest minutes-document style. Reads like a printed minute. Zero visual weight, but can wrap if there are many opposed.
MOTION:
THAT Council approves a 4.5% water rate increase effective July 1, 2025.
MOVED BY: Councillor Johnston
SECONDED BY: Councillor Wall
OPPOSED: Councillor Rabel
Carried
2

Red outlined name chips

Each opposed councillor gets their own outlined chip. Scannable at a glance — easy to count without reading.
MOTION:
THAT Council approves a 4.5% water rate increase effective July 1, 2025.
MOVED BY: Councillor Johnston
SECONDED BY: Councillor Wall
Opposed Rabel Woodill
Carried
3

For / Against tally pills

Mirrors the disposition pill. Two small pills side-by-side: total FOR and total AGAINST. Names appear quietly after, or on hover.
MOTION:
THAT Council approves a 4.5% water rate increase effective July 1, 2025.
MOVED BY: Councillor Johnston
SECONDED BY: Councillor Wall
4 FOR 1 AGAINST Rabel
Carried
4

Councillor dot-matrix (initials)

MOTION:
THAT Council approves a 4.5% water rate increase effective July 1, 2025.
MOVED BY: Councillor Johnston
SECONDED BY: Councillor Wall
C J W Wd R
4–1 · Opposed: Rabel
Carried
Tiny colored squares (one per councillor, initial inside). Whole council is visible. Hover reveals full name.
5

Expandable "1 opposed ▸" chip

Collapsed by default — a subtle red pill with a count. Click to expand and reveal names. Compact when most motions pass unanimously.
MOTION:
THAT Council approves a 4.5% water rate increase effective July 1, 2025.
MOVED BY: Councillor Johnston
SECONDED BY: Councillor Wall
1 opposed
Carried
MOTION:
THAT Council approves a 4.5% water rate increase effective July 1, 2025.
MOVED BY: Councillor Johnston
SECONDED BY: Councillor Wall
OPPOSED: Rabel, Woodill ▾
Carried
6

Stacked initial avatars

Overlapping circular avatars — green for yes, red for opposed. Feels like a voting "team photo." Subtle summary text beside.
MOTION:
THAT Council approves a 4.5% water rate increase effective July 1, 2025.
MOVED BY: Councillor Johnston
SECONDED BY: Councillor Wall
C J W Wd R
Opposed: Rabel
Carried
7

Split bar with counts

A tiny horizontal stacked bar (green/red) visualizes the split at a glance. Compact count label beside. Feels infographic-y.
MOTION:
THAT Council approves a 4.5% water rate increase effective July 1, 2025.
MOVED BY: Councillor Johnston
SECONDED BY: Councillor Wall
4–1 · Rabel
Carried
8

Minus-prefix name list

Each opposed name with a red "−" sign in front. Hybrid of text and chip — quiet but distinctly flagged.
MOTION:
THAT Council approves a 4.5% water rate increase effective July 1, 2025.
MOVED BY: Councillor Johnston
SECONDED BY: Councillor Wall
Opposed Rabel Woodill
Carried
9

Inline vote-breakdown grid

Mini two-column grid: FOR / AGAINST with the names beside each. Reads like a formal roll-call without being a full table.
MOTION:
THAT Council approves a 4.5% water rate increase effective July 1, 2025.
MOVED BY: Councillor Johnston
SECONDED BY: Councillor Wall
For
Caisley, Johnston, Wall, Woodill
Opp
Rabel
Carried
10

"View vote" ghost button

Neutral button with a red dot + count. Click to expand the card and reveal the full breakdown inline. Minimal footprint, hides detail until needed.
MOTION:
THAT Council approves a 4.5% water rate increase effective July 1, 2025.
MOVED BY: Councillor Johnston
SECONDED BY: Councillor Wall
Carried
