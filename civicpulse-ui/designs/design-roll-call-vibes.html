Roll Call — 10 Design Vibes

All 10 options share the exact card from the app (white · 1px #e2e8f0 border · radius 10 · padding 12×14) plus the standard MOTION: / MOVED BY: / SECONDED BY: lines and the green CARRIED pill on the lower-right. Only the roll-call treatment varies. Sample vote: 4 in favour · 1 opposed (Rabel).

1

Two plain lines — IN FAVOUR / OPPOSED

Mirrors MOVED BY / SECONDED BY exactly. No new styling introduced.
MOTION:
THAT Council approves a 4.5% water rate increase effective July 1, 2025.
MOVED BY: Councillor Johnston
SECONDED BY: Councillor Wall
IN FAVOUR: Mayor Caisley, Councillor Johnston, Councillor Wall, Councillor Woodill
OPPOSED: Councillor Rabel
Carried
2

Single "VOTE:" summary line

One compact line with count + names. Same mrow styling as MOVED BY. Minimum footprint.
MOTION:
THAT Council approves a 4.5% water rate increase effective July 1, 2025.
MOVED BY: Councillor Johnston
SECONDED BY: Councillor Wall
VOTE (4–1): Caisley, Johnston, Wall, Woodill in favour; Rabel opposed.
Carried
3

Roll-call list — one councillor per line

Each councillor on their own line in the exact MOVED BY style, with a colored IN FAVOUR / OPPOSED token.
MOTION:
THAT Council approves a 4.5% water rate increase effective July 1, 2025.
MOVED BY: Councillor Johnston
SECONDED BY: Councillor Wall
Mayor Caisley: IN FAVOUR
Councillor Johnston: IN FAVOUR
Councillor Wall: IN FAVOUR

Councillor Rabel: OPPOSED
Carried

Nested ROLL CALL sub-box

A quiet gray sub-box inside the card — same radius/border treatment as the sub-item wrap already in the app.
