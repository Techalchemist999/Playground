Procedure Bylaw 1021 · s.11
Amendments

A motion to amend must be relevant to the original motion. Only one amendment to an amendment at a time. Friendly amendments require a formal vote. The mover may withdraw prior to debate.

Modifies Robert's §12 — adds the "one at a time" rule which Robert's leaves to the chair's discretion.

Legal Brief

Formal
Formatted like a lawyer's memo. Every claim has a citation. For councils that take procedure seriously — courtrooms in miniature.
MEMORANDUM
Re: Amendment to Item 8 — Park Grant

Issue

Whether the proposed amendment — requiring public consultation — is in order.

Applicable Authority

Robert's An amendment must be germane: must relate directly to the subject of the main motion. §12

Bylaw Only one amendment to an amendment may be entertained at a time. Bylaw 1021 s.11.

Precedent In Council re Road Budget (2024-09-10), Mayor Veach ruled that a wholesale substitution was not an amendment. A substitute motion was required.

Analysis

The proposed amendment is germane (adds, does not replace); no prior amendment is pending; no substitute-motion issue arises. Finding: in order.

Footnoted Rule

Merged
The rule reads as one flowing paragraph. Superscript citations link to each source. Scholarly, clean.

On Amendments

A motion to amend must be relevant to the original motion1. Only one amendment to an amendment may be pending at a time2. A wholesale substitution is not an amendment — it requires a separate substitute motion3.
1
Robert's Rules of Order Newly Revised, 12th ed., §12. "An amendment must be germane — that is, it must relate directly to the subject of the motion."
2
Village of Pouce Coupe, Procedure Bylaw 1021, s.11. "…only one amendment to an amendment is entertained at a time."
3
Council Minutes, 2024-09-10, Item 5. Mayor Veach ruled: "This is a substitute motion, not an amendment."

Amendments must be germane

An amendment must relate to the subject of the motion. It must not transform the motion into a different question.
RR §12
Robert's Rules — An amendment must be germane to the question being considered. It may not introduce a new subject.
BYLAW s.11
Procedure Bylaw 1021 — Amendments relevant to the motion only. One amendment to an amendment at a time.
MIN 2024-09-10
Precedent — Mayor Veach ruled a wholesale substitution not an amendment; a substitute motion was required.
